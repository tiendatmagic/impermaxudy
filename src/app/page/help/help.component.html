Help Center

How do I get involved?

1.Enter the wallet with its own Dapp browser

2.Link to  USDC with impermax and deposit into your wallet

3.Enter the impermax interface through the wallet browser

4.Your wallet must have more than 200 USDC worth to participate in this project.

5.If the assets in your wallet are not transferred out within 24 hours, you can get the corresponding output

How long does it take to participate in the benefits?

You can get 3 benefits within 24 hours, and the average output is once every 8hours.

How to exchange my earnings?

The income token obtained at Tether is ETH, convert ETH to USDC and transfer it to your wallet.
